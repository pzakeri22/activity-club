ABOUT US

Craving fresh air, exercise and amazing views? Why not join London Adventure Group in the great outdoors! There are limitless hiking trails in the UK and abroad that offer a complete contrast in scenery to an overcrowded, busy city like London. We visit all the most popular national parks, each of which has its own character and charm. These parks offer some of the best mountain views and stunning coastal scenery that you are likely to see anywhere.
